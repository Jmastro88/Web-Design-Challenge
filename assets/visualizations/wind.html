Wind Speed

As you can see, wind speed seems to increase as you reach a higher Latitude (40-80 Deg. Latitude). This makes sence if you consider to amount of land mass in this area. The more land mass the more stations to record wind speed and the more terrian that could help intensfy the condistions for wind. Most of all the worlds tornados are in this Latitude range.
